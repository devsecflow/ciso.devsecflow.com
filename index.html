Leading Modern Security Programs

A 4-Day Executive Course for CISOs and Security Leaders

This comprehensive course equips senior security professionals to navigate the evolving landscape of cybersecurity. With a focus on resilience, AI risk governance, and modern engineering practices, the program prepares CISOs to lead security transformation across complex, high-stakes environments.

Who Should Attend

  • Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs)
  • Deputy CISOs and Heads of Security Risk or GRC
  • Security Leaders in regulated or critical sectors

Key Outcomes

  • Build operational resilience across systems and teams
  • Govern AI systems and manage emerging technology risks
  • Lead secure-by-design transformations with engineering rigour
  • Communicate effectively with boards and regulators

Course Curriculum

1

Resilience-Driven Security Leadership

Theme: Operational Resilience as a Business Imperative

  • Redefining security for resilience
  • Business continuity, system recovery, and third-party risk
  • Accountability and measurement at the executive level
  • Workshop: Build your Resilience Readiness Framework
2

Governing AI Risk in Cybersecurity

Theme: AI-Augmented Security, Risk, and Governance

  • Common AI failure modes: hallucination, drift, misuse
  • AI lifecycle governance: transparency, validation, risk registers
  • Use of AI in SOC operations: detection, triage, automation
  • Workshop: Create an AI Risk Register and governance map
3

Engineering for Resilience with Chaos Principles

Theme: Designing Secure Systems that Degrade Gracefully

  • Introduction to security chaos engineering
  • Simulating failure in authentication, IAM, detection systems
  • Observability and recovery metrics
  • Workshop: Chaos Engineering Test Design
4

Executive Simulation & Strategic Planning

Theme: Practising Strategic Security Leadership

  • Incident response simulation: 24-Hour Executive Clock
  • Board-level communication and leadership under pressure
  • Strategic planning and delivery frameworks
  • Deliverables: Executive Brief + 90-Day Resilience Plan
